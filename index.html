The experience

From hello to heirloom

01

Say Hello

Tell us who you are and what you're celebrating. Send a note with your date, your vision, and the people who matter, and we'll reply with availability, pricing, and a few thoughtful questions to get to know you. There are no wrong answers here, just the start of a good conversation.

02

The Session

We show up early, chase the golden light, and let the day breathe. As a married team, we work in tandem so nothing is missed, the two of us watching for the glance, the laugh, the quiet in-between. We'll guide you when you need it and step back when the moment is already perfect. Mostly, we want you to forget the camera is there.

03

The Reveal

After the session we hand-edit every frame in our warm, natural-light style, no rushed presets, no shortcuts. Your private online gallery arrives ready to relive, download in full resolution, and share with everyone you love.

04

Keep It Forever

Order heirloom prints, albums, and wall pieces straight from your gallery, made on archival paper meant to outlast the screen. Some moments deserve to hang on a wall and be passed down. We'll help you choose the ones that should.

The studio

The two behind the lens

Edwin & Joyce — Austin, Texas

We're Edwin and Joyce Ormeo, a husband-and-wife team who have spent nearly twenty years photographing love, light, and the people brave enough to be themselves in front of us.

Twenty years ago, photography was Edwin's calling and a camera was how we first learned to see the world the same way. Marriage made us partners; the work made us a team. Since then we've stood at the back of countless ceremonies, knelt in golden fields, and chased the last warm light of the day side by side. We shoot as one, which is why you'll always hear us say "we" — there are two of us reading the room, two of us anticipating the glance, the laugh, the quiet hand-squeeze you didn't know we saw.

Our family is the heart of all of it. We're raising three kids — Ethan, Lizzie, and Enzo — and they're the reason we believe so deeply in slowing down for the people and moments that matter. Faith roots us and gratitude steadies us, and both shape the way we show up: present, unhurried, and genuinely glad to be there. When Edwin isn't behind the camera, he's teaching the next generation of commercial photographers — because a craft this good is meant to be passed on.

Recently we packed up a life well-loved in Northeast Florida and planted roots in Austin, Texas. Now the Hill Country is our backyard — those open fields, big skies, and gentle evening sun-flare that make our kind of warm, natural-light work feel like home. We photograph weddings, couples and engagements, families, high school seniors, lifestyle and branding portraits, and the events worth remembering — here in Austin and wherever the road (or the light) calls us.

You're in Austin, but do you travel?+

We do, gladly. We're based in Austin and know the Texas Hill Country light by heart, and we're always happy to follow a story beyond it. We spent many years photographing in Northeast Florida before moving here, so destination work feels like home to us. Tell us where you're headed and we'll talk travel.

How do we book you?+

Send us a note with your date and what you have in mind. Once we know it's the right fit, a signed agreement and a retainer hold your day on our calendar, the rest is paid before your session. We only take a limited number of bookings each season so we can give every couple and family our full attention, so reaching out early is always wise.

When will we see our photos?+

You'll receive a few favorites within a week as a first taste. Full galleries are typically ready within three to four weeks, and weddings within six to eight. We hand-edit every image ourselves, so we never rush the part that makes them yours, but we'll always keep you in the loop along the way.

What should we wear?+

Think soft, timeless, and like yourself. Earth tones, creams, and gentle textures glow beautifully in golden-hour light, and we love when families and couples coordinate rather than match. Once you've booked, we'll send a simple styling guide and we're always a text away if you're deciding between two outfits.

Do you offer prints and albums?+

Yes, and we believe in them deeply. Every gallery includes the option to order archival prints, heirloom albums, and framed wall art crafted to last for generations. A photo you can hold is a different kind of memory than one on a screen, and we'll help you choose pieces worth passing down.
